Our Services

1

Individual Debt Collection

Individual debt collection is the process of recovering outstanding funds from individuals who have not repaid their debts on time. These debts may include personal loans, credit cards, unpaid bills, and other financial obligations.

2

Corporate Debt Collection

Corporate debt collection is the process of recovering money owed to a company by its clients or suppliers who have failed to pay their debts on time. These debts may include unpaid invoices, contract dues, or loans. The process includes offering flexible payment solutions such as debt installments.

3

Bank Debt Collection

This is the process of recovering money owed to banks by customers who have not paid their loans or financial obligations, such as personal loans, mortgages, credit cards, or other debts. This service includes several steps, beginning with contacting the debtors to remind them of payment, and offering flexible solutions .

4

Vehicle Repossession

Vehicle repossession is a service carried out by banks, insurance companies, or financial institutions to reclaim vehicles from owners who have failed to pay due installments or debts related to the car. This service usually takes place after a series of warnings or notices have been issued to the debtor to settle the outstanding amounts.
